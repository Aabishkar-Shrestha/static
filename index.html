Chef's specialties

Chicken Tikka Biriyani

Chicken tikka is a popular dish where tender chicken chunks are marinated in a blend of aromatic spices and yogurt, then grilled to perfection, resulting in juicy and flavorful bites.

Potato Fry

Potato fry is a tasty and versatile dish where sliced potatoes are shallow-fried until crispy on the outside, with a soft and tender interior, enhanced with a mix of aromatic spices.

Gapao Rice

Gapao rice, also known as Thai basil fried rice, made with stir-fried rice, aromatic holy basil, and a combination of meat or seafood, resulting in a satisfying and fragrant meal.

Nankotsu karaage

Nankotsu karaage is a Japanese dish consisting of deep-fried chicken cartilage that is crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, often served as a tasty and crunchy appetizer or snack.
